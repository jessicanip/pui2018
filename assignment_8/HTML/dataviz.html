Visualizing Environments

_

Speculative Design • Visual Communication
Individual Project
2018

How do we explore alternatives and
trajectories of the future?

The Future of Health + Commute explores a health-centric world that is heavily embedded within our increasingly automated commutes.

This project aims to spark conversation through a glimpse into the day-in-the-life of a commute to work — the environment, things, people and the interactions that exist in this future scenario of widely accessible and personalized health experiences. Full process documentation can be found here.
