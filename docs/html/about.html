Hi, I'm Jessica —


I am a designer seeking to create experiences beyond physical products and interfaces. Currently studying Design + Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University.


I perceive design as a way to communicate how we envision the world. Currently, I’m exploring the relationship between two- and three-dimensional worlds, using a human-centered approach towards speculative design and design fiction, and thinking about the impact of my work in the greater context of the world. I’m interested in conceptual design thinking within the fields of communication, spatial, and service design.


Please feel free to reach out at jessicanip@cmu.edu.
